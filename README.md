Tamagatchi
==========

Progamming challenge from /r/dailyprogrammer.

The Tamagatchi pet can be fed, poop, exercise contract diseases, and fall in love.
It is your job to take care of your Tamagatchi!

Main methods:

- feed :      +5 food
- poop :      +5 poop
- exercise:   +5 health
- sleep:      +15 health
- clean_poop  -5 poop
